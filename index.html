Home Dashboard

Privacy Policy — Last updated April 1, 2026

Home Dashboard is a personal iPad kiosk app. This policy describes what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data accessed on your device

Home Dashboard accesses the following on your device, solely to display information on screen:

  • Calendar — to show today's events
  • Photos — to display a slideshow from your photo library
  • Reminders — to display your shopping list
  • HomeKit — to show smart home device status and camera feeds
  • Local network — to connect to HomeKit devices and Plex media server on your network

None of this data is transmitted off your device by the app.

Third-party services

  • Apple WeatherKit — your configured location (latitude and longitude) is sent to Apple's WeatherKit API to fetch weather data. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Unsplash — weather condition keywords are sent to Unsplash to fetch background photos when no local image is available. This is governed by Unsplash's Privacy Policy.

Data storage

App settings (weather location, WiFi credentials, dashboard title) are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. No data is synced to the cloud or shared with any third party.
